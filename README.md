# ai-crossroads-claude
Riding your motorcycle, race across those roads. Written with Claude 4.6.


## Overview
You control a motorcycle, traveling from the bottom of the screen to the top, then back from top to the bottom. You are passing 8 lanes of freeway to get there. Cross the freeway as many times as you can within the 3 minute timer.

The motorcycle starts on the left lane at the bottom of the screen. Use the up and down buttons to move. When you cross all 8 lanes and get to the other side of the freeway, the motorcycle turns around and it starts at the top of the right lanes. 

There are 9 levels to the game. Each level lasts 20 seconds. For each level, there are a certain number of cars going down the freeway at a certain speed. When the game moves to the next level, the speed and traffic in lanes increases.

If you get hit by a car, your motorcycle returns to the start of its lane. This game doesn't have "lives" - in this game, it only ends when the three minutes pass.

Everytime you cross the freeway, you get points. You get points that match the level. For example, if you pass the freeway on Level 4, you get 4 more points.

The current level is shown in the upper-left corner. The score is in the upper-right corner. When all 9 levels are completed, the game is over. Press the space bar to play again.


## Details
This game was inspired to play like an Atari game (1200 & 2600) from the 80's.  There a multiple games from that era that have a "cross the screen" theme. The graphics here are nicer than from back then, but to an extent are in line. Atari technical challenges of that era impacted this design. For example: lots and lots of horizontal movement and limited vertical movement? That's Atari.
